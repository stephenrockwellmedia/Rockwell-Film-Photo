How It Works

Three simple steps

1
📱

Scan the Code

Place a QR code card on every table at the reception. Guests scan it with any smartphone — no app download needed.

2
🖼️

Browse the Gallery

They land on a live gallery showing every photo and video uploaded that evening — growing in real time all night long.

3
✨

Add Their Own

Tap the gold button, pick a photo or video from their camera roll, write a personal message, and it appears instantly for everyone.

♡

What's Included

Everything you need

⚡

Live & Instant

Photos and videos appear in the gallery the moment they're uploaded. No waiting, no delays — your guests see each other's moments as the night unfolds.

💬

Personal Messages

Every upload includes a personal message from the guest — displayed right on the photo. Like a digital guestbook attached to every single memory.

📷

Photos & Videos

Guests can upload photos or videos directly from their camera. Candid moments, dance floor videos, heartfelt speeches — all in one place.

🔗

No App Needed

Works on any smartphone — iPhone or Android. Scan, tap, upload. Your guests won't need to download anything or create an account.

📦

Yours to Keep

The gallery stays live for 30 days after your wedding. Before it closes, you receive a full download of every photo and video your guests uploaded — delivered straight to you.

🎨

Beautifully Designed

Built to match the elegance of your day. Dark, cinematic, and refined — the same aesthetic as your wedding photography and film.
