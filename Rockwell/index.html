From Our Hearts

A Letter to Our Brides

Hey — can we talk to you for a second? Not as a business. Just as someone who has spent the last 8 years showing up for couples on the most important day of their lives.

We know you've been planning this for a long time. The venue, the flowers, the dress, the seating chart — you've thought about every single detail. And somewhere in the middle of all of that, you started wondering about your photos and film. You want them to be beautiful. You want them to feel real. You want to watch your highlight film ten years from now and still feel everything you felt that day.

We get it. Because we've sat across from hundreds of couples just like you, and we've heard the same thing every time — "We just want to remember how it felt."

"We just want to remember how it felt."

That's exactly what we're here for. When you book with Rockwell Film & Photo, you're not just hiring a videographer and photographer. You're bringing someone into your family for a day — someone who genuinely cares about you, who will laugh with you during portraits, who will stay calm when things run late, and who will make sure you never feel like you're performing for a camera.

We've been doing this for over 8 years. We've shot weddings in the rain, in the snow, in 95-degree heat. We've dealt with last-minute venue changes, missing boutonnieres, and ceremonies that started an hour late. And through all of it — every single time — we've come home with something beautiful. Because the love is always there. And that's what we capture.

You deserve to be fully present on your wedding day. You deserve to dance without worrying about the camera, to cry without feeling self-conscious, to laugh so hard your stomach hurts and know that someone is there catching all of it.

That someone is us.

With love,
Stephen & the Rockwell Team

Meet Stephen

We tell love
stories honestly

Stephen discovered his passion for wedding film and photography in 2016 after capturing his first "I do" — and he's been hooked ever since. Driven to preserve life's most meaningful moments, he went on to earn a Bachelor's in Multimedia Technologies in 2018. Years of travel, dedication, and hands-on storytelling have shaped him into a true artist behind the lens.

Today, Stephen leads a tight-knit team of photographers and filmmakers who share his vision and standard of excellence. Together, they deliver a seamless, stress-free experience and breathtaking visuals that authentically tell your story.

With meticulous attention to detail, a creative eye, and a genuinely heartfelt approach, Stephen is devoted to preserving the magic of your day — beautifully, honestly, and forever.

Stephen Rockwell Lead Filmmaker & Founder
